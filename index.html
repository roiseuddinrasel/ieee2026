About the Conference

Welcome to ETIC-2026

Dedicated to exploring the latest advancements in emerging technologies, engineering, electronics, and electrical engineering — since 2021.

ETIC Since 2021
28 Days Published in
IEEE Xplore

ETIC-2026 is an IEEE-approved international conference that invites researchers, academics, industry professionals, and policymakers across EEE, physics, applied engineering, and smart solutions to share insights and foster collaborations.

ETIC-2026 is approved by IEEE to publish proceedings in IEEE Xplore, indexed in Scopus at their discretion. Since 2021, our previous conference proceedings have been published in IEEE Xplore within just 28 days.

This conference serves as a discovery tool, a collaboration tool, and a visibility tool — giving early-career scholars and doctoral students a prestigious international platform.

Publication & Review

Your Research Deserves Global Reach

Every accepted and presented paper enters a prestigious global archive accessed by millions of researchers worldwide.

  • IEEE Xplore Publication
    Proceedings published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library within 28 days of the conference.
  • Scopus Indexing
    Papers indexed in Scopus database at IEEE's discretion, maximizing visibility and citations.
  • Rigorous Peer Review
    All submissions undergo a double-blind peer review process by international experts.
  • Presentation Required
    Only papers that are presented will be submitted to proceedings — ensuring a high-quality event.
  • IEEE Author Rights
    Authors retain rights to distribute and post their accepted manuscripts with proper attribution.
  • Microsoft CMT3 System
    Submissions are managed through Microsoft's CMT3 research management platform.
